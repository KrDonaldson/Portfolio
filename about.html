A little about me

I enjoy the basics like building clean user interfaces and prototypes, building, coding, and deploying projects with responsive designs, and monitoring and updating those projects as needed.
But I love the challenge of learning something new and developing my skills.

Some history

I didn't set out to code. In college I majored in mathematics at a small college. One of my required courses was cancelled due to low enrollement numbers and I was forced to either wait a year or go to a different school. I decided to turn this setback into an opportunity and took on a second major - Computer Science. Math was my passion so I put my focus on that in University while taking on Customer Service roles, until the opportunity arose to take a Mean Stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular and Node) bootcamp and I was reminded how fulfilling coding was. From there, I studied the MEAN stack for a few months before moving onto fullstack development.

The ultimate goal

I am a dreamer and humanitarian at heart. I want to persue a career where I am developing my skills while creating and building projects that help others.
